# Woven Web Site Architecture Document

## 1. Site Map & Navigation Structure

### Primary Navigation
- Home
- About
- Participate
- Donate

### Footer Navigation
- Mission & Vision
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Social Media Links
- Contact Information

### User Flow Patterns
1. Community Engagement Path
   - Home → Participate
2. Support Path
   - Home → About → Donate

## 2. Page Inventory

### Homepage
- **Purpose**: Introduce organization's mission and current initiatives
- **Key Messages**: Connection, community, conscious technology
- **Primary CTA**: Get involved
- **Sections**:
  - Hero with mission statement
  - Impact statistics
  - Community stories
  - Newsletter signup
- **Media**: High-quality photos from events

### About Page
- **Purpose**: Share organization story and values
- **Key Messages**: Origins, mission, impact
- **Primary CTA**: Donate
- **Sections**:
  - Origin story
  - Mission & vision
  - Team profiles
  - Impact metrics
- **Media**: Team photos, impact infographics

### Participate
- **Purpose**: Convert visitors to active community members
- **Key Messages**: Ways to participate and contribute
- **Primary CTA**: Sign up
- **Sections**:
  - Ways to participate
  - Volunteer opportunities
  - Current initiatives
- **Interactive Elements**:
  - Volunteer application form
  - Initiative signup

### Donate
- **Purpose**: Enable financial support
- **Key Messages**: Impact of donations
- **Primary CTA**: Donate now
- **Sections**:
  - Donation options
  - Impact stories
  - Financial transparency
- **Interactive Elements**:
  - Donation platform
